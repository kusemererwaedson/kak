About Us

TIGPE builds and manages agriculture projects and enabling infrastructure and services through investment, propagation and plantation, and processing.
Our investment platform, Rubiz, is approved and regulated to serve ordinary people, high-net-worth individuals, corporates and institutions. Rubiz, regulated by Uganda Capital Market Authority, is an Ethical Private Capital Marketplace platform that focuses on sustainable and impact-driven crowd-investments and financing.

We are best known for our impact investment Rolling Fund which has been operational since 2022. Rubiz offers access to investment opportunities in distinctive and promising businesses while also serving as a platform for entrepreneurs to showcase their ventures to a community of global investors.

TIGPE propagation and plantation operations is set up to produce tissue culture plants of different species of plants covering Bamboo, Horticultural plants, Forestry trees, Herbal Plants, Ornamental Plants and Agricultural Plants that are free from diseases. We support farmers through extension services to enable best agronomic practices for improved production and productivity.

Our processing operation envisages to develop state-of-the-Art infrastructure & technology in the agro-processing industry and facilitate end to end linkage, from the farmer to the consumer. It aims to provide a mechanism to link agricultural production to the market by bringing together farmers, processors and retailers so as to ensure maximizing value addition, minimizing wastage, increasing farmers income and creating employment opportunities particularly in the rural sector.

We believe that planting in collaboration with smallholder benefit their families and communities, and the environment too. Growing bamboo-based agroforestry protects soil from erosion and degradation, captures carbon, and helps prevent the destruction of local, indigenous forests. By turning underutilized land into a network of thriving farmlands and microforests, we can make a massive environmental impact, as well as a profound impact on the lives of the people whose lives are rooted in the land.

We are currently doing trials of different bamboo-based agroforestry models at our Nursery in Wakiso Town Council, Wakiso and Pilot Plantation in Apoi, Apac. The bamboo-based agroforestry models will be developed along group farming systems for scale and improved production and productivity, enabling small holder farmers to increase their revenues and be more climate SMART and market resilient.
