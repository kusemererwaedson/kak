Ethco = Ethical + Collaborative

Through the TIGPE Investment platform, Ethco, we raise funds from investors to invest alongside us in local businesses.


We didn't set out to become investors.

We started our business in 2014 to provide quality planting materials to farmers, and before we knew it, we had hundreds of farmers needing financial support. Along the way, we approached financial institutions to lend a hand, but it was always a miserable experience.


We asked ourselves why financial institutions dragged out deals for months and renegotiated over and over again and still not deliver. It was horrible. Then we read about Musharakah that facilitates billion-dollar opportunities quickly by keeping the process painless and straight forward. Nobody had done this for smallholder farmers like us, so we decided to do it ourselves.


In 2020, we incorporated TIGPE and started collaborating with farmers as Partner, Shareholder or Leaser using a simple one-month contracting process, then leaving the farmers alone to do their thing while we oversee.

It turned out we were right, farmers loved it.

Enterprise Value

We started Ethco to become the financier we wish could have supported us.

We thought about all the stuff we didn’t like about dealing with financial institutions and worked backwards. It turns out, they really do make it too complicated and there are tons of farmers like us, who wanted a long-term partner for their agribusinesses.
